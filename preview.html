Welcome to FitWit

FitWit is a comprehensive health and fitness tracking application built with React Native for iOS and Android devices. This app aims to provide holistic health management by integrating with fitness wearables, Apple HealthKit, and Google Fit for real-time data synchronization.

Features

Health Tracking

Track a wide range of health metrics including nutrition, water intake, exercise, mood, and sleep.

Smart Integration

Seamlessly integrates with fitness wearables, Apple HealthKit, and Google Fit for real-time data sync.

AI Assistant

AI-powered chatbot assistant that provides personalized health suggestions and insights.

Personalized Insights

Get personalized health recommendations based on your unique health patterns and goals.
